Tips for Effective Time Management

1. Create a list of things you need to do. Doing this ensures that you don't forget anything and being able to cross tasks off after you've done them can give you a sense of accomplishment, further motivating you.

2. Prioritization plays a large role in time management. Figuring out what is more important and completing those tasks first helps you work more efficiently. Play my game "The Perfect Balance" to see how prioritization can keep you from becoming overwhelmed! In this game, a Girl Scout student has to balance her sleep, grades, and social life.

3. Create a schedule for yourself. This will keep you on track during the day since you know what you have to do and when you have to get it done by. If you don't follow the schedule you created, you might feel guilty which further motivates you to work.

4. Take breaks from time to time. Don't try to overwork yourself. That could lead to more burnout and exhaustion, making yourself feel even less productive.

5. Try to stop multi-tasking. Multi-tasking can trick your brain to think that you are accomplishing a lot when in reality, you aren't.
